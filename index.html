My Cats

Luna Kamhaji

I have two cats at home named Ash and Max. Ash is 6 months old, and Max is 5 years old.
Until last spring, we had a black cat named Zoro who passed away from lymphoma.
Due to his death, Max unfortunately fell into a depressive state where he would refuse
to eat food. Seeing this, we made the difficult descision to adopt a kitten in order for
Max to have company.

Ash

We adopted my kitten Ash when he was around 1 month old.
His litter was found abandoned by their feral mother in a construction site
by a young couple in Brooklyn.

Although he was a bit timid when he first arrived to our house,
he has now grown to be a friendly, agile, and playful cat who enjoys
the presence of people.
He eats almost everything, to the point where I can't leave food out around him.

He loves to sit in very confined spaces, enjoys getting attention, and
loves to eat food. He is more on the extroverted side and will sometimes cuddle
I believe that he can entertain himself with anything around him and
is very curious.
Some of the things I find him playing with are :

  • Pieces of string
  • Bottlecaps
  • Tissue/Toilet paper
  • Wires
  • My hair

Max

We adopted Max from a cat cafe in Brooklyn sometime in 2019 when he was around
one year old. He was very playful and curious, but when my other cat Zoro passed away
Max was very distraught and started to refuse any food and only wanted to sleep.
After we adopted Ash, he has become a bit more energetic.

Max was very skinny and fragile during his first couple of years with us.
He used to be a stray and was found with a spinal injury, which he got surgery
for and fortunately recovered, although he has always been a bit clumsy especially
in his back legs/lower body. For some reason, recently he suddenly gained a lot of weight
and we were told he is overweight by the vet.
He is a very friendly and curious cat, but it is a bit rare for him to cuddle.
